Projects

Aether

Play as a lonely black hole, drifting through space and gobbling up other space debris. As you consume more and more, you will continue to grow in size. This game was made over 24 Hrs as part as a friend-coordinated game Jam. There are plans to update it in the near future to address lingering bugs, general gameplay wonkiness, and update a few assets, but no longer-term development is planned.

  • Unity
  • C#
  • Blender
